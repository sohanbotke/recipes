ingredients:
1. tamarind paste - 2 tablespoon
2. mint chutney - 1 tablespoon
3. papadi - 10 pieces
4. potato - 1 boiled
5. onion - 1 small
6. tomato - 1 small
7. indian shev - 1 cup
8. whole milk yogurt 
9. red chilli powder
10. cumin powder

procedure:
1. mix tamarind and mint chutney , with 2 tablespoon of water.
2. crush boiled potato, add red chilli powder , salt and cumin powder. mix well.
3. finely chop onion and tomato.
4. spread 8 papadi on plate, top them off with the mixed potato.
5. using a spoon, add the mixed tamarind and mint paste on each.
6. dilute the yogurt a bit, add salt and put 1 spoon on each papadi.
7. garnish with finely chopped onion and tomato. 
8. top off with the delicious shev and some chilli powder if you like it hot.
